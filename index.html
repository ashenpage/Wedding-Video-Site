Our Service

We create memories, that wont want to be forgotten.

  • PLANNING
  • EQUIPMENT
  • DELIVERY

We let you know straight away if we are available for you set dates; choose your desired package, then the dates, and our site instantly lets you know if we are available for that time.

Preparation is important for us, to understand your selected venue and how the day is expected to play out, so we request to come along to the rehearsal to sketch out the best wedding video for you for no extra fee.

If we are not able to make it to the rehearsals, we request images of the venue, so we are able to sketch out the area to be able to envision the best film for you.

On shoots we provide our own equipment and one man cameraman, so no need for the extra cost of renting out equipment. We like to use up to three cameras (depending on requirements) to capture the best moments of your special day, while being discrete.

We use high quality cameras shooting in HD. Our main camera is a DSLR 'Canon 600D', which we use to shoot with to get the best look for your wedding, we have our cameraman shoot the DSLR on a shoulder-rig to get the smoothest shots, to make you special day as fluid as possible. While our second camera the Canon S120 is put on a tripod out of the way, being able to capture the full performance. Then we use a GoPro to capture beautiful wide angle video, for a back and for the stylised extra angle.

With this equipment we are able to portray a beautiful story of you day, with the use of many different angles, producing a beautiful cinematic video.

We specialise in music video wedding videos, which means we really focus on making you an amazing "music video-like" production that you'll want to watch again and agian.

WE deliver the video in HD 720p (or 1080p if requested) for high quality playback, to re-live the every detail.

We upload the video to Youtube, unlisted so only our clients can view the video, as privacy is priority.
